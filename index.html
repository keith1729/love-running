Why Love Running With Us?

Feel Healthy

Since it improves aerobic fitness, running is a great way to improve your cardiovascular health. Plus, it burns calories and can build strenght.

Feel strong

Running strenghtens your heart by pumping blood more efectively through your body.

Feel free

Physical exercise, running included, is known to increase endorphines in the brain. Endorphines make you more energetic, more awake and yes happier.

Get social

When we run with others we run farther and faster more easily. Social running can pull you out of running funk or low mood, and inspire you to try new events.
